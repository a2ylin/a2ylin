## About me:

My name is Aylin Manafzade, and I am 19 years old. I am an aspiring Data Analyst and QA Engineer with the motivation to become an IT professional. I am a senior Finance student at the Azerbaijan State University of Economics. I have always been eager to learn more about Data Analytics and the IT world so I first started developing my skills in Oracle Database SQL and Microsoft Excel by attending Data SoCool courses and working on real-world projects. I am a Team Member of the IT Development Team at the European Youth Parliament Azerbaijan while also interning at a1qa as a QA Functional Tester. In addition, I am developing my Data Analytics skills as a Data Analyst Intern at Devlab LLC. I represented Azerbaijan at the International Session of the European Youth Parliament in Pordenone, Italy, where I applied my problem-solving, critical thinking, communication and leadership skills. Moreover, I gained valuable customer service experience at Qalan AZ, where I worked as a Math Mentor. I am always ready for new challenges and eager to apply my technical skills in the IT industry.
